## Status Key
- **applied** — Submitted, waiting to hear back
- **screening** — Recruiter screen scheduled or completed
- **interviewing** — In active interview loop
- **offer** — Received offer
- **rejected** — Did not advance
- **withdrawn** — You withdrew from the process

## Rules
- Maximum 2 active applications per company at any time.
- After every outcome, fill the outcome log and propagate learnings.
- Review this tracker weekly to follow up on stale applications.
