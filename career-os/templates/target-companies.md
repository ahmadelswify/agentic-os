## How to Use

1. Each week, open every career page below and search for your target role keywords.
2. When a matching role appears, run the go/no-go filter in `examples/workflows/job-search.md`.
3. If it passes, create `Applications/[company]/` and start the resume-tailoring workflow.

## Selection Criteria

Companies listed here should pass at least two of these tests:

- **Skill match.** Your specific combination of skills is rare on their existing team.
- **Domain match.** You've worked in their industry or an adjacent one.
- **Stage match.** Their growth stage produces the kind of role you want (e.g., early-stage = generalist, late-stage = specialist).
- **Location match.** Office is in a city you're willing to work in, or they're remote-friendly.
