# Interview Preparation Workflow

## Trigger
User has an upcoming interview and asks for help preparing.

## Steps

### 1. Review the Application
Read the application folder for this company:
- The JD and fit assessment
- The resume version that was sent
- Any notes from previous rounds

Understand what version of yourself they've seen.

### 2. Review Learnings
Check `Learnings/` for:
- Communication patterns that work for this type of role
- Interviewer type patterns (if known)
- Items on the never-use list
- Domain vocabulary that resonates

### 3. Select Stories
Pick 3-5 STAR stories that map to the role's priorities:

| Priority | Story | Why | Algorithm Label |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [story] | Maps to their #1 stated requirement | [label] |
| 2 | [story] | Addresses a gap or concern | [label] |
| 3 | [story] | Shows leadership or scale | [label] |
| 4 | [story] | Backup for unexpected questions | [label] |
| 5 | [story] | "Tell me about yourself" anchor | [label] |

### 4. Practice Mode
For each selected story:
1. Have the user deliver the 90-second executive lead
2. Check for:
   - Algorithm label stated upfront
   - "I" language (flag any "we")
   - Clear metric at the end
   - Under 90 seconds
3. Run through 2-3 probe questions
4. Give specific feedback

### 5. Company Research
If the user wants, research the company:
- Recent news, funding, product launches
- Company culture signals from the JD and website
- The team or org this role sits in
- Why this role likely exists (growth, backfill, new initiative)

### 6. Prepare Questions to Ask
Help the user draft 3-5 thoughtful questions:
- At least one about the team's current challenges
- At least one about what success looks like in 6 months
- Avoid questions easily answered by the company's website

### 7. Create the Prep Doc
Fill out `templates/interview-prep.md` with all the above.

### 8. Post-Interview Capture
After the interview, prompt the user to fill in:
- What was asked
- How they answered
- What went well
- What to improve
- Interviewer signals

Then update:
- Story outcome logs
- Learnings (any new patterns)
- Application tracker
