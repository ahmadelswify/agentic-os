# How career-os Works

## The Career Flywheel

career-os is built around a flywheel: every job application makes the next one stronger.

```
Impact Library → Tailored Resume → Interview → Outcome
      ↑                                          |
      └──── Learnings ← Outcome Log ←────────────┘
```

### Phase 1: Build Your Foundation

Before applying anywhere, build your **Impact Library** and **STAR Stories**.

**Impact Library** captures every meaningful achievement with:
- What you did (the action)
- The measurable result (the metric)
- The context (team size, constraints, timeline)
- Alternate framings (how this sounds for a PM role vs. an SE role vs. an ops role)

**STAR Stories** are your interview arsenal. Each story is a reusable module with:
- The core narrative (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Probe answers for follow-up questions
- Role-type variations (which details to emphasize for different audiences)

### Phase 2: Tailor and Apply

When a job description arrives:

1. **Parse** the JD into structured requirements
2. **Score** your fit across 5 dimensions
3. **Map** each requirement to your best matching achievement
4. **Translate** your experience into the target company's vocabulary
5. **Rewrite** the resume JSON with tailored bullets
6. **Verify** single-page constraint and writing rules
7. **Generate** the PDF

### Phase 3: Prepare and Interview

Before each interview:
1. Review the JD and your tailored resume
2. Select 3-5 stories mapped to the role's priorities
3. Practice with algorithm labels and 90-second leads
4. Research the company and interviewer

### Phase 4: Capture and Learn

After every interview or outcome:
1. Capture what was asked and how you answered
2. Update story outcome logs (what worked, what didn't)
3. Add learnings (new patterns, phrases that landed, things to avoid)
4. If hired: celebrate and update your Impact Library with the new role
5. If rejected: extract the lesson and propagate it forward

### Why It Works

Most job seekers start from scratch every time. career-os compounds your effort:
- Stories get better with practice and feedback
- Domain translations build a vocabulary library
- Learnings prevent repeating mistakes
- The Impact Library grows with every achievement, making future tailoring faster
