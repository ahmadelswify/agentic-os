# Prep Meeting

Help the user prepare for a meeting by reviewing history and surfacing open items.

## How to Do This

1. Ask who the meeting is with (if not specified)
2. Review People/ files for past history
3. Check for overdue action items involving attendees
4. Review relevant decisions
5. Draft suggested agenda
6. Surface risks

## Important

- Show open commitments both ways
- Ask "Anything you want to make sure to bring up?"
- Never show file paths or metadata
