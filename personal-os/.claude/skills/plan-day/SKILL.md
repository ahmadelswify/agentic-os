# Plan My Day

Review tasks across all life areas and suggest 1-3 focus items for today.

## How to Do This

1. Read goals for current priorities
2. Find P0 tasks and anything due today
3. Check for blocked tasks
4. Select up to 3 items from different life areas

## Important

- Never more than 3 unless asked
- Explain WHY each was chosen
