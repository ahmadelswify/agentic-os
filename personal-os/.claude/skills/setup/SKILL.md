# Set Up personal-os

Guide the user through creating their life management system.

## The Conversation (3 rounds)

1. **Life areas**: "What areas do you want to manage? Career, relationships, health, finances, projects?"
2. **Priorities**: "For each area, what's your top priority right now?"
3. **Current load**: "Brain dump everything on your plate."

## After the Conversation

Create: GOALS.md, INBOX.md, Tasks/ subdirectories, Knowledge/ directory

Suggest: `/process-inbox`, `/plan-day`, `/add-task`

## Important

- About 5 minutes
- Never show file paths or YAML
