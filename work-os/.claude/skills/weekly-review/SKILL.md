# Weekly Review

Help the user reflect on their week and plan the next one. Should feel like a supportive check-in.

## How to Do This

1. Find everything completed or updated this week
2. Check goals to see which areas got attention
3. Review upcoming deadlines and priorities

## What to Present

- **This Week's Wins**: Celebrate accomplishments
- **Progress Update**: Where projects and goals stand
- **Patterns Worth Noting**: Only if genuinely useful
- **Next Week's Focus**: 2-3 priorities
- **Quick Housekeeping**: Tasks to archive or re-prioritize

## Important

- Be encouraging but honest
- Never show file paths, metadata, or priority codes
- Keep it concise
- Suggest logging significant accomplishments to career tracker
