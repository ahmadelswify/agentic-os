# Prep for Meeting

Help the user walk into a meeting prepared.

## How to Do This

1. Ask who the meeting is with (if not specified)
2. Search for history with this person
3. Pull relevant recent accomplishments
4. Find open tasks or blockers involving this person
5. Check for commitments from past conversations

## What to Present

- **Recent Wins to Share**
- **Updates to Give**
- **Things to Ask About**
- **Follow-ups from Last Time**

## Important

- Keep talking points conversational
- Never show file paths or metadata
- Tailor to the relationship type (manager 1:1 vs. client vs. team standup)
- Ask "Anything specific you want to bring up?"
